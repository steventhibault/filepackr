filepackR DEMO REQUEST FORM
===========================

The two Request a demo buttons now open a popup form.

Submissions are sent to:
steve@stevethibault.com

IMPORTANT FIRST-TIME ACTIVATION
-------------------------------
After uploading the site, submit one test request.
FormSubmit will send an activation email to steve@stevethibault.com.
Click the activation link before expecting live submissions to arrive.

The popup is responsive on mobile and includes required validation,
success and error messaging, click-outside close, Escape-key close,
and a hidden honeypot field for basic spam protection.
