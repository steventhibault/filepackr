FAQ

Onboarding automation questions.

Is this only for HR teams?

No. The same model can support any team that repeatedly scans intake packets, personnel documents, credentialing files, or compliance records.

Can staff still decide what goes where?

Yes. The goal is to reduce repetitive handling while keeping staff involved in exception review and final approval.

Can this support custom naming conventions?

Yes. The workflow can be shaped around your preferred document names, employee folder structure, and review rules.
