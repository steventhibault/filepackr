The workflow

Built for offices where paperwork still arrives messy.

Many teams do not start with clean digital files. They start with stacks of forms, signatures, IDs, authorizations, and supporting records that have to be scanned and organized consistently.

1

Scan the paper stack

Staff scan the paperwork into a working file or watched scanner folder.

2

Review suggested records

filepackR identifies pages, groups related documents, and flags uncertain or poor-quality scans.

3

Route final files

Approved records are named consistently and sent to the correct employee, client, case, or workflow folder.

FAQ

Paperwork automation questions.

What is paperwork automation software?

It reduces repetitive document handling after scanning, including recognition, grouping, naming, routing, and review.

Does filepackR replace staff judgment?

No. It prepares suggested outputs and highlights exceptions so staff can review before final files are created.

Can filepackR support custom document types?

Yes. Recognition rules, expected documents, filenames, and workflow templates can be configured around the organization.
